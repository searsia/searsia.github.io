About

Today's web search engines need to combine search results from many independent sources, such as results from a Web crawl, from Advertisements, from Video's, Books, News, Shopping, etc. Searsia provides an approach to search that:

  • Manages and shares large collections of independent sources.
  • Selects for each query the most relevant sources.
  • Combines sources in an aggregated search interface.
  • Learns over time what kind of information each source provides.

Searsia is free and open-source software, and demonstrated by UTwente Search, and Dr Sheet Music.

UTwente Search is an enterprise search engine for the University of Twente that combines the results of more than 40 sources, including results from Google's web crawl, from Courses, from News, the Telephone directory, the Timetables, as well as results from social media, such as Facebook, Twitter and Flickr.

Dr. Sheet Music is a federated search engine dedicated to sheet music. It combines the results of more than 50 resources, including advertisements from affiliate programs like Amazon, eBay and ShareASale and advertisements for charities like CharityChoice and GoedeDoelen.nl.

Privacy Policy

Searsia does not collect or share personal information.

At Searsia.org, we don't know who you are and there is no way to tie your activity on the site to you as a person. We do not store Cookies, nor other identifying information that is automatically sent by your web browser like your browser's User agent or your IP address. We do not use third party tracking software like Google Analytics. We do not allow third party Cookies to be stored when you use Searsia.org.

Searsia.org is hosted at a third party (Github) that has its own independent privacy policy. We have no responsibility or liability for the information that Github stores or shares.
